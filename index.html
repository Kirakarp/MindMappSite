About MindMapp

MindMapp is a website that recommends you the best places close to you that are perfect for meditation. It's perfect for anyone who wants to practice a little more self-care in a relaxing environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

MindMapp can help anyone who wishes to try out meditation and recommends the best places to try. Plus, with MindMapp, you can discover new places and parks that are great places to visit for self-care.

We will give you tips and tricks to help you get started at meditating and make the most out of your experience with MindMapp! But don't worry if it doesn't work out right away. The more you do it, the easier it will get. And remember, your meditation time is for you :D

MindMapp is also a great way to keep track of how much time you give yourself for self-care. Each time you use MindMapp's Meditation Map, you can gain points. If you gain enough points, you can earn yourself badges. These badges should help you remember that taking care of yourself and meditating should be a rewarding experience!
